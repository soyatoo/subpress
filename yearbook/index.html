About the yearbook

A yearly collection of pictures and stories that showcase the global exploring community's creativity and insanity. It captures who was around and what we did - the parties, the daring infiltrations, the cruisy explores, and everything in between. I tend to prefer candid shots. To maximize your chances of being included, read these guidelines and ask questions if needed. Going through past Yearbooks will also give you an idea of what I like, as due to submission volume and page count, I simply can't include everyone.

Due to the nature of content published, the yearbook’s audience is kept on a strictly vetted basis. Please do not share submission details or the book itself unless you’ve asked first.

Photo requirements

  • Taken between 1st of Jan 2024 and 31st Dec 2024
  • Up to 100 images
  • Visibly exploring-related
  • People in frame
  • Photos you have personally taken
  • Phone / DSLR / Film photos are all fine
  • 1000px minimum on the largest side
  • Faces uncensored

Faces are blurred on request, but I like to make them all the same style. Images that accompany a story do not need to include people.

Story requirements

  • As a file, eg. Google Doc / Word / Notepad;
  • Max 1,500 words;
  • No names or location details if it's spicy;
  • Up to 2 stories per contributor;
  • At least one image per story (doesn't need to include people in frame).

If you've got a story to tell but aren't a confident writer, get in touch and we can workshop something.
