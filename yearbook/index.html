Please note that as much as I'd love to include everyone, due to the volume of submissions I simply can't. The yearbook shouldn't be viewed as a vessel for any individual to view their work, rather a time capsule of the entire community. To maximise your chances of being included, make sure you read these guidelines and ask questions if need be.

What is the yearbook?

A yearly collection of pictures and stories that showcase the global exploring community's creativity and insanity. It captures who was around and what we did - the parties, the daring infiltrations, the cruisy explores, and everything inbetween - rather than amazing pics of people standing in tunnels (though a few of those do get in). Continue reading the guidelines to know what you should submit.

Photo requirements

* If you write something about the image it may be included without these.

- Taken between 1st of Jan 2023 and 31st Dec 2023;

- Visibly exploring related*;

- People in frame*;

- Faces unblurred (they'll be blurred on request but I like to make them all the same style!);

- Photos you have personally taken! Or tell me who took it and I'll ask them if I can use it;

- Phone / DSLR / Film photos are all fine, but plz not tiny resolution (1000px + on one side absolute minimum);

- As many photos as you like, I might enjoy a pic that you're iffy on, or dislike one you love. Hedge your bets.

* If you write something about the image it may be included without these.

Story requirements

- Exploring related;

- A google doc/word/notepad file so I can store it and not forget (as happens with email);

- If you have pics with your story, send them in the place you think they'd best sit and we can workshop;

- No names or location details if it's spicy;

- No more than 2 stories per contributor.

Ordering

Price: $60 AUD + shipping

Softcover, 280+ pages, max 200 copies printed.

Everyone who submits has a book put aside for them by default, but if you need to order multiple copies or aren't submitting, send an email to lodge a pre-order.
